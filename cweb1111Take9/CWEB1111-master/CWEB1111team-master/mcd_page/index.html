McDonald's Menu Data

McHelping You Get Informed

Our Mission!

Our goal is to present our visitors with enough information on the food they consume from their favorite restuarant! The data is pulled from "url" gathered since "year" and its initial intent was to document all nutrition info of McDonald's menu items. We believe this information can help in various situations such as measuring calorie consumption, carb consumption, serving size, etc.

Charts

*There was multiple items with the same count or with zero in which case we used the lowest with calories.

  • The first chart is a doughnut chart which displays the total amount of calories on the McDonald's menu, based on 6 categories: Breakfast, Lunch, Salads, Sides, Desserts, and Drinks.

  • Our second chart (Bar chart) displays the menu item with the most calories in each category.

  • Finally, the third horizontal bar chart displays the menu item with the least amount of calories for each section.
